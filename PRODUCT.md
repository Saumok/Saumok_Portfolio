# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Hiring managers and tech recruiters (primary — 30-second first impression, need fast navigation to projects/experience/contact and a CV), senior developers/tech leads (secondary — will probe technical depth, GitHub links, and try to break the UI), and curious peer developers (tertiary — explore every interaction and easter egg). Source: portfolio/docs/prd.md §2.

## Product Purpose
A cinematic, multi-chapter interactive portfolio for Saumok Kundu ("The Architect of Intelligence") — an AI/ML architect. Goal: be the one portfolio out of 50 that a hiring manager remembers, engages with for 5+ minutes, and shares. Demonstrates by example that Saumok builds things both functional and beautiful.

## Brand Personality
Cinematic hacker-OS fiction: terminal boots, classified dossiers, neon violet/cyan on near-black void, gamified exploration (XP, achievements, access challenges). Confident, playful, technically deep.

## Anti-references
The standard hero/about/skills/projects/contact template portfolio. Anything forgettable, static, or CMS-shaped. Not a blog, marketplace, or social platform.

## Design Principles
- Every chapter is a self-contained world with its own visuals and interactions.
- Show, don't tell — technical depth is demonstrated through working interactive systems.
- Desktop is the primary cinematic experience; mobile is a graceful, fully functional fallback (2D treatments replace WebGL).
- Exploration is rewarded — nothing hidden should be boring.
- Reduced motion and accessibility fallbacks are mandatory (design.md §10).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
prefers-reduced-motion alternatives for all animation; prefers-contrast: high token overrides; focus-visible outlines; ARIA roles on dialogs/status; recruiter "skip" bypass on the gamified contact gate.
